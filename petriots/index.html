F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Our veterinary clinic offers a range of services to help keep your pets healthy and happy. These services include routine check-ups, vaccinations, spaying and neutering, dental care, diagnostic testing, surgery, and emergency care. We also provide nutritional counseling, behavior consultations, and pet boarding services. Our team is dedicated to providing compassionate care and building long-lasting relationships with both you and your pets. Please don't hesitate to contact us if you have any questions or would like to schedule an appointment.

We recommend bringing your pet in for a check-up at least once a year. However, the frequency of visits may vary based on factors such as age, health, and medical history. Senior pets or those with chronic conditions may require more frequent check-ups. Our veterinarian will work with you to develop a personalized wellness plan for your pet, which includes a recommended schedule of check-ups and preventative care. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to ask our team.

We offer two methods to contact our veterinary clinic in case of an emergency. You can either dial our emergency contact number, 0912345678, to speak with a member of our team 24/7 or use our mobile app to communicate with us directly. Our staff takes emergencies seriously and responds quickly. If you have any questions or need further information about our emergency procedures, please don't hesitate to contact us.

We offer two convenient ways to make an appointment with one of our veterinarians. Walk-ins: You can visit our clinic during our regular hours of operation and we will do our best to fit you in with one of our available veterinarians. Mobile App: Alternatively, you can download our mobile app, which allows you to schedule appointments, view your pet's medical records, and communicate with our veterinary team from the comfort of your own home. We understand that your time is valuable, and we strive to make scheduling an appointment as easy and convenient as possible. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Our veterinary clinic operates from 9 am to 6 pm, Monday through Friday. We are closed on weekends and major holidays. If you need to schedule an appointment outside of our regular hours of operation, please let us know and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.

During your pet's visit to our veterinary clinic, you can expect a comprehensive physical examination from one of our experienced veterinarians. They will check your pet's vital signs, eyes, ears, mouth, skin, coat, and overall body condition. Depending on the reason for the visit, they may also perform diagnostic tests such as blood work, urinalysis, or X-rays. Our veterinarian will discuss their findings with you and recommend any necessary treatments or medications. You can also expect our team to provide compassionate care and answer any questions you may have about your pet's health and well-being.
